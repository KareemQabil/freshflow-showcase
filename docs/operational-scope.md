# FreshFlow Operational Scope

This note expands the public-facing operational scope of the FreshFlow showcase.

## Publicly visible operational modules

The screenshots currently prove three important ERP surfaces:

- orders
- suppliers
- purchase orders

These are useful public proof points because they sit close to core business operations and show more than one isolated page type.

## Broader system direction inferred from source inspection

Additional modules appear to exist behind the scenes:

- inventory and stock movement
- drivers and settlement
- expenses and reports
- settings and brand configuration
- documents and RBAC
- ZATCA-facing surfaces

Those are not all exposed publicly yet because the current goal is to publish strong, safe proof without over-exposing raw product internals.

## Public showcase principle

This repo should show breadth without pretending to be a full documentation dump. The public layer is strongest when it:

- shows real UI
- explains the module relationship
- credits collaboration honestly
- avoids unsupported technical claims
